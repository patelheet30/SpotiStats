Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 13th September 2023

Information Collection

Your privacy is important to us. This website doesn't collect or store information, or use third-party services that collect information. It also doesn't track you or use advertising or analytics services.

How we use your information

The website uses your information to generate a thorough webpage to view your Spotify use. All data provided is sent to the server, processed, and then sent back to you. Any data that is not provided will not be shown on the webpage and default to None/0.

Cookies and other technologies

The website uses cookies to merely track if you did provide the data. It does so to ensure that the website can function properly. It allows you to reload the page without having to re-enter your data. It also ensures that you don't have to re-enter your acknowledgement of the privacy policy.
